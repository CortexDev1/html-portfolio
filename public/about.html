About Me

I utilized advanced lab equipment, such as spectrum analyzers and oscilloscopes, for precise measurements and in-depth analysis, guaranteeing the performance and reliability of communication systems. I also collaborated effectively with cross-functional teams and communicated technical findings and recommendations clearly and concisely.

Prior to joining NIWC Pacific, I was a Product Engineer at Advantech, where I skillfully managed product life-cycles for multiple product lines, overseeing critical aspects such as pricing, availability, and peripheral compatibility. I collaborated closely with sales personnel and customers to identify their unique hardware and software requirements and proposed tailored solutions that met their specific needs and aligned with Advantech’s product offerings and roadmaps. I led the development of customer-specific solutions by providing pre-sales and post-sales technical support, conducting feasibility verifications, preparing product proposals, and overseeing project management throughout the development process.
